Welcome to the Catholic Community of the Samui Archipelago. Whether you are a local resident or a visitor to our beautiful islands, we invite you to join us for Holy Mass. We provide current schedules for Catholic Mass in Koh Samui at St. Anna’s in Nathon and Mary Help of Christians in Chaweng—which offers a weekly Sunday Mass in English. We also serve the growing Catholic community in Koh Phangan near the Haad Rin area. Find our mass times, locations, and direct contact links below.
